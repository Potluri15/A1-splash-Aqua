PP-Pleated Micron filter Cartridges

Our Water Purification Systems provide the best solutions for industrial purposes. They are designed to deliver clean and safe drinking water.

Details

A Pleated Polypropylene (PP) Micron Cartridge Filter is a filtration device designed to remove fine particles and contaminants from liquids. It features a pleated design, which increases the surface area for filtration, leading to higher dirt-holding capacity and longer service life.

Advantages

  • 1. Product is made of Superfine PP Fibrous Membranes for absolute filtration
  • 2. Outer core, end caps and inner core are fused by heat welding. No adhesives used
  • 3. Factory Sterilized
